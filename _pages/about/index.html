Welcome to my homepage!

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) at Columbia University. My research interests lie in optimization, game theory, machine learning, optimal transport and social network.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Professor Michael I. Jordan and associated with the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) group. From 2023 to 2024, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratory for Information & Decision Systems at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, working with Professor Asuman Ozdaglar. Prior to this, I received a B.S. in Mathematics from Nanjing University, a M.S. in Pure Mathematics and Statistics from University of Cambridge and a M.S. in Operations Research from UC Berkeley.
